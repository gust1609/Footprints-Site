FAQ

Answers to common questions

What is Footprints?

Footprints is an application that helps runners see activities and visualize coverage of the areas they've explored.

Do I need a Strava account?

Yes. Footprints syncs with your Strava account to import runs and display coverage. You'll be prompted to connect your account when you first sign in.

How often does the app sync with Strava?

The app synchronizes your latest runs whenever you open the map screen. A nightly job also updates all connected accounts.

Why am I not seeing my latest run?

Synchronization can take a few minutes. Make sure your Strava activity is set to public and refresh the map screen to trigger a manual sync.

Can I recalculate my coverage manually?

Yes. You can trigger a manual recalculation from the sync Strava activity if you believe coverage is out of date.

Who can access my data?

Your run data is private to your account. Footprints only stores the information necessary to display your coverage and does not share it with third parties.
