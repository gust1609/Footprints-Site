Privacy Policy

Your data, your control – always

Footprints respects your privacy and is committed to transparency. We use the Strava and Garmin APIs to import your running activities only after you authorize the app through their official, secure login flows.

How We Handle Your Data

  • 🏃‍♂️
    Activity Access: We only access your public running activities to calculate and visualize your running coverage across city districts. Your private activities remain private.
  • 🔒
    Privacy Control: We do not access or store private activities unless you explicitly authorize access in Strava or Garmin Connect settings.
  • 🚫
    No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or any external services. It stays within Footprints.
  • 📊
    No Profiling: Your data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purposes beyond the core app functionality.
  • ⚙️
    Full Control: You can disconnect your accounts at any time via your Strava or Garmin Connect settings. Your choice, always.
🛡️

Our Promise

All activity data is used exclusively within the Footprints app to display your runs, statistics, and achievement badges. Nothing more, nothing less.
