Support

Help, privacy, and contact information for the iOS scorekeeper for the physical Gabon card game.

Contact

For support, bug reports, App Store review questions, or privacy requests, email:

gustav.manthorpe@gmail.com

What To Include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • The Gabon app version.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • What happened instead.

For privacy requests, include "Privacy" in the subject line and describe what you need. Do not send passwords, recovery codes, or other sensitive personal data.

Privacy

Gabon Scorekeeper does not require an account and the submitted App Store build keeps scorekeeping data on device.
