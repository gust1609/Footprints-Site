Privacy Policy – Footprints

Footprints uses the Strava and Garmin APIs to import your running activities after you authorize the app via their official login flows.

Here is how we use your data:

  • We access your public running activities to calculate and visualize your running coverage across city districts.
  • We do not access or store private activities unless you explicitly authorize access in Strava or Garmin Connect.
  • Your data is never shared with third parties.
  • Your data is not used for advertising or profiling.
  • You can disconnect your accounts at any time via Strava or Garmin settings.

All activity data is used only within the app to display your runs, stats, and badges.

For questions or data deletion requests, please contact gustav.manthorpe@gmail.com.
