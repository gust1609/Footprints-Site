🏃‍♂️ Footprints

Footprints is a mobile app that visualizes how much of your city you’ve explored by foot using your Strava and Garmin activities.

  • Connects with Strava and Garmin to import your runs
  • Displays your coverage across city districts
  • Earns you badges for exploration and running milestones

The app complies with Strava and Garmin's branding and API guidelines:

  • ✅ Official login flows ("Connect with Strava", "Sign in with Garmin")
  • ✅ “Powered by Strava” and “Garmin Connect compatible” branding
  • ✅ Links to original activity sources where applicable
