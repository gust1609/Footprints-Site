🏃‍♂️ Footprints

Footprints is a mobile app that visualizes how much of your city you’ve explored by foot using your Strava activities.

  • Connects with Strava to import your runs
  • Displays your coverage across districts
  • Earns you badges for achievements

This app uses the official Strava API and includes proper attribution as required:

  • ✅ “Connect with Strava” button
  • ✅ “Powered by Strava” logo
  • ✅ “View on Strava” links
