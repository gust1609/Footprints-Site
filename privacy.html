Privacy Policy – Footprints

Footprints uses the Strava API to import your running activities after you authorize the app via Strava's OAuth flow.

Here is how we use your data:

  • We access your public running activities to calculate and visualize your running coverage across city districts.
  • We do not access or store private activities unless you explicitly allow it in Strava settings.
  • Your data is not shared with any third parties.
  • Your data is not used for advertising or profiling.
  • You may disconnect your Strava account at any time.

All activity data is used only to display statistics and badge achievements within the app.

For any questions or data deletion requests, contact gustav.manthorpe@gmail.com.
